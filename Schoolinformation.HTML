Activities


The school organizes Annual Day, Annual Sports Day, and sports events. Students represent the school in inter-school activities including sports, drawing competitions, elocution and essay writing competitions, dance and quiz competitions, and scouting & Guiding.

The School Alumni team organised a Golden Jubilee Celebration called '"SWARNARPAN"' in 2016 inviting the Alumni`s,Primary and Secondary School teachers.

Campus


The school building had two floors. The ground floor consists of 8 classrooms and Primary school Principal office. The first floor consists of Secondary Principals cabin and an office with around four class rooms. It also includes Audio visual class, Science laboratory, Computer lab, and a library.[citation needed]

The second floor includes seven class rooms, of which three are recently built. Grounds are not present within the school premises because of space limitations but it includes a long jump pit and high jump has been played in premises as well; the adjacent Municipal Ground is used for the Sports and Annual Sports Day activities. Tilak Nagar police station is adjacent to the school premises.

The able Captains who steered this Marvellous Vessel of Knowledge over the years have been- Sr. Baptista, the first Head Mistress (our School did not have Principals then.) Mother M.Theresa, Mother M.Damian, Sr. M. Frances, Sr. M.Stanislaus, Sr. M.Scholastica, Sr. M.Francine and at present Sr. M.Jyothsna.
