Student Behaviour

  • 1. At the first bell i.e. 5 minutes before the meeting commences all must take their allotted places. Here the prayers before class will be said after which all will go in silence to their respective class-rooms.
  • 2. While changing classes, when girls move along the corridors, they must walk in silence.
  • 3. Running, playing or shouting inside the school building is not allowed.
  • 4. No mobile phones, expensive watches, gold chains, any thread or band on the hands or neck will be allowed.
  • 5. Any breach of discipline and above all discourtesy and disrespect to teachers in any way will be taken seriously.
  • 6. The school will not be responsible for goods, money or books lost by students.
  • 7. A notice of two days must be given for any certificate or document required from the school office.
  • 8. Students must take part in the school co-curricular activities, be they regular or occasional i.e. dramatics, display, excursion, picnics, rallies or social services.
  • 9. Parents may engage the teachers of the institution for private tuitions only after the permission of the Principal / Headmistress is obtained. No gift should be given to teachers.
  • 10. No pupil will be called to answer phone calls during school hours.
  • 11. No collection for any purpose, whatsoever, is to be started without the previous permission of the Principal.

Our expectations

In our school we expect every student:
  • To be always friendly with one another in and out of school.
  • To avoid vulgarity in your talk and behavior.
  • To accept whatever work is assigned to you as your rightful share.
  • Not to sit and wish the Teachers or any Authority who enters or passes through the room / corridors in which you happen to be.
  • To respect the liberty and the rights of other pupils.
  • To learn and observe good manners at the table and elsewhere.
