Welcome to Parag Higher Secondry School Ujjain

“Winning isn't everything, but wanting to win is.”

Parag Higher Secondry School family has created and managed some of the India’s greatest educational institutions like Parag Higher Secondry School (P.H.S.S.). These school are city top school in Commerce and Arts respectively.
“ Our endeavor is to develop our students into all-rounder personalities who can excel in all walks of life. ”
Parag Higher Secondry School Ujjain (CBSE school) is an institution conceived to impart the best in education along with development of values and personality traits that groom achievers. Our pedagogy, curriculum and infrastructure are carefully designed to develop life skills, such as logic, reasoning and creativity in all our students.
